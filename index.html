1. Thinking

Design is not just about visual. I know it’s a cliche to say that, but I totally agree with that. Before any kind of design, I listen and search why it have to be designed first. Knowing the needs of the design helps me make a better design strategy.

2. Design

I love design only less than my family, friends and my pet. I think I am not a nature-born genius designer, but I do try my best to polish and conquer myself year by year. I try diversity of design, but currently target at Web, and UI/UX design.

3. Coding

I do HTML5/ CSS3 and javascript. On the way to somewhere deeper like objective c or C++, but most of time I use language for building websites. I also got some experiences with jQuery and Drupal.
